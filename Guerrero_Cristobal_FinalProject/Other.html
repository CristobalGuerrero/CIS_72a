Video Game Backround

Yeah thats a mouthful so mmorpg is a whole lot better then saying all that at one time. There is a good list of those mmo's that are quite popular these days such as World Of War Craft though the one I play is from Dev Cat which is distributed online by Nexon that is called Mabinogi. Not really though they tend to have greater boost in power and have more potential to grow in stats.
